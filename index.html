About Me

Before beginning her journey to become a web developer, Laura worked for twenty five years as a Mechanical Engineer in the defense industry. She worked on a variety of programs ranging from SINCGARS radios to test eqipment for optical devices to cabling for GPS satellite panels.

In her free time, Laura enjoys exploring the Austin area, where she has lived for almost a year. Her other hobbies include taking road trips, cooking, spending time with her family, and reading. She also creates jewelry and decorative pieces using stained glass and fused glass.
